BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

I will join in School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University, in August, 2024. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Operating System Lab, Peking University in 2024, advised by Prof. Ding Li, Prof. Yao Guo and Prof. Xiangqun Chen. Before graduated from Peking University, I obtained a bachelor's degree from Southeast University advised by Prof. Shi jin(IEEE Fellow) and a master's degree from Cornell University advised by Prof. Zhiru Zhang(IEEE Fellow). I also closely work with Dr. Yuxin Ren at Huawei Technologies who is the head of academic research of Huawei Euler operating system.
I work on Efficient and Secure EDR Systems, which covers a wide range of research topics including operating system security, provenance analysis, hardware offloading, malware detection and AI security. Specifically, my goal is to make EDR Systems robust,secure and effieicnt to detect and resolve security, performance, and runtime issues in enterprise environments.
I'm always looking for highly self-motivated master students and undergraduate interns (RAs) to work with me. Please directly send your CV to me if you find my research interesting.
