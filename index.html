BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

I will join in School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University, in August, 2024. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2024, advised by Prof. Ding Li, Prof. Yao Guo and Prof. Xiangqun Chen. Before graduated from Peking University, I obtained a bachelor's degree from Southeast University advised by Prof. Shi jin and a master's degree from Cornell University advised by Prof. Zhiru Zhang.
I work on Efficient and Secure EDR Systems, which covers a wide range of research topics including system audting, provenance analysis, hardware offloading, malware detection and AI security. Specifically, my goal is to make EDR Systems robust,secure and effieicnt to to detect and resolve security, performance, and runtime issues in enterprise environments.
I'm always looking for highly self-motivated master students and undergraduate interns (RAs) to work with me. Please directly send your CV to me if you find my research interesting.
